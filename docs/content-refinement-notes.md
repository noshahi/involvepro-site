# Content Refinement Notes (Future Pass)

Once the full site is structurally complete, we need a dedicated copy/content
refinement pass. The service detail pages are structurally complete but some
pages may still feel too similar or too AI-generated in tone. Later, review
every service page and improve:

- shorter, sharper headings
- more human and direct copy
- clearer client pain points
- more natural agency voice
- less repetitive title structure
- stronger differentiation between similar service pages
- better proof points
- more client-focused messaging
- stronger internal linking
- AEO/GEO blocks where relevant
- better FAQ wording
- more trust-building examples

## Goal of the future content pass

Make the site feel like a real agency speaking directly to the visitor,
understanding their needs, and guiding them to the right next step.

Do not execute this full copy rewrite now. Only document it for later.
